## Project Description

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard developed to analyze Vrinda Store's sales performance for the year 2022. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, customer demographics, order status, top-performing states, and sales channels. Using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers, the analysis helps identify key business patterns and supports data-driven decision-making.







# Business Questions & Insights

## 1. Which Month Got the Highest Sales and Orders?

**Answer:**
March recorded the highest sales and order volume.

- Highest Sales: ~1.92M
- Highest Orders: ~2,900

This indicates that March was the best-performing month for the business.

---

## 2. Who Purchased More – Men or Women in 2022?

**Answer:**

- Women: 64%
- Men: 36%

Women contributed significantly more to total purchases, making them the primary customer segment.

---

## 3. What Were the Different Order Statuses in 2022?

**Answer:**

| Order Status | Percentage |
|-------------|------------|
| Delivered | 92% |
| Cancelled | 3% |
| Returned | 3% |
| Refunded | 2% |

The majority of orders were successfully delivered, reflecting strong operational performance.

---

## 4. Which Are the Top 5 States Contributing to Sales?

**Answer:**

| State | Sales Amount |
|---------|-------------|
| Maharashtra | ~2.99M |
| Karnataka | ~2.65M |
| Uttar Pradesh | ~2.10M |
| Telangana | ~1.71M |
| Tamil Nadu | ~1.68M |

Maharashtra generated the highest sales among all states.

---

## 5. What Is the Relationship Between Age and Gender Based on Number of Orders?

**Answer:**

- Adult Women contributed the highest number of orders.
- Women placed more orders than men across all age groups.
- Adults were the most active customer segment, followed by teenagers and seniors.

This suggests that adult women are the key target audience for the business.

---

## 6. Which Sales Channel Contributed the Maximum Revenue?

| Channel | Contribution |

| Amazon | 35.48% |
| Myntra | 23.36% |
| Flipkart | 21.59% |
| Ajio | 6.22% |
| Nalli | 4.78% |

Amazon was the highest-performing sales channel, contributing over one-third of total sales.

---

## 7. What Are the Key Business Insights from the Dashboard?

**Answer:**

- Women contributed 64% of total purchases.
- March recorded the highest sales and order volume.
- Maharashtra was the top revenue-generating state.
- Amazon was the leading sales channel with 35.48% contribution.
- Adult women represented the largest customer segment.
- 92% of all orders were successfully delivered.

These insights can help the business optimize marketing campaigns, inventory planning, and channel strategies.
